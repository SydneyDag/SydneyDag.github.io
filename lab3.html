Dolphins are incredibly intelligent and playful creatures! They use echolocation to navigate and hunt, producing clicks and listening for the echoes that bounce back. They can recognize themselves in mirrors, a sign of self-awareness, and even have unique whistles, like names, to identify each other. Dolphins also sleep with one eye open and half of their brain awake to stay alert for predators. Known for their acrobatic skills, they can jump up to 20 feet out of the water. With their friendly nature and complex social structures, dolphins are truly fascinating inhabitants of the ocean!

  1. Dolphins can swim at speeds of up to 20 miles per hour
  2. Dolphins can live for several decades
  3. There are around 90 different species of dolphins
